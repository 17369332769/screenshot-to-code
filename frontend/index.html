FAQ

Questions people ask before trying Image to Code.

Can this generate production-ready code?

It works best as a fast first pass you can preview, edit, and refine before shipping.

What kinds of inputs work best?

Product screenshots, website references, Figma exports, wireframes, and short text prompts all work well depending on the workflow.

Can I keep editing the generated result?

Yes. The generated result is meant to be revised and iterated on, not treated like a frozen export.

Who is this best suited for?

It is useful for founders, designers, product teams, agencies, and engineers who want to move from reference visuals to a working front-end draft faster.
